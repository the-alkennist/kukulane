Kukulane Privacy Policy

Welcome to Kukulane, a platform connecting farmers and buyers to streamline transactions and reduce costs associated with intermediaries.

At Kukulane, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users' information. Here's how we collect and use data:

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location, and payment details to facilitate communication between farmers and buyers, process transactions, and improve our services.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal information such as browser type, operating system, device information, and IP address for analytics and website optimization purposes.

2. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the confidentiality and integrity of your information. These measures include encryption, firewalls, secure socket layer (SSL) technology, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

3. Third-Party Services

Kukulane may utilize third-party services, such as payment processors, analytics providers, and advertising partners, to enhance our platform's functionality and user experience. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information, and we encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal data.

4. Cookie Policy

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. By using Kukulane, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with our Cookie Policy.

4.1 What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They enable the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you donâ€™t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

4.2 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To recognize you when you visit our website and authenticate your access to certain features or areas.
  • Preferences: To remember your preferences and settings, such as language and region.
  • Analytics: To collect information about how you interact with our website and analyze traffic patterns to improve our services.
  • Advertising: To deliver personalized advertisements based on your interests and browsing behavior.

4.3 Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer, and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site, and some services and functionalities may not work.

4.4 Cookie Consent

By using Kukulane, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree to the use of cookies, you should adjust your browser settings accordingly or refrain from using our website.
